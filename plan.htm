Mission Statement

The goal of the website is to provide the highest quality, and most comfortable clothes for kids, and to attract them to buy our clothes, and participate in our activities. Then, we should let customer know our brand: FRIAS.

Success Evaluation

We will calculate the ratio of clothing sales and site visits, and evaluation of customers, to determine the needs of customers.

Target Audience

This site will be designed for kids. We will show different styles of clothing, so that children find their favorite products, which includes cute, colorful, anime style, which attracted them to participate in our fashion show.

Content

  • Homepage:To introduce our kids fashion store: FRIAS, which include explain our purpose, to promote our vision, and to tell consumers our mission.
  • Products page: Show the pictures of our products to attract consumers to participate in our activities. Then make a connection to online order form.
  • Café page: Show the pictures of the coffee shop and promotional information, and provide the date of opening.
  • Events page: List of events in chronological order.
  • Services page: Show our value-added services, including free shipping information. And the production of online feedback form, thus improving our shortcomings. And then show us the contact information and address.
